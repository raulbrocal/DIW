Recognized by critics as one of the most talented and versatile actors of his generation. He was developing small roles and a fixed intervention on the Saturday Night Live show, until he managed to make a name for himself among the young stars of the eighties.

In addition, he acted in Golpe al sueño americano (1987), Natural Born Killers (1994), Prodigious Youngsters (2000), Kiss Kiss Bang Bang (2005), Fur: An Imaginary Portrait of Diane (2006), Zodiac (2007), Iron Man (2008), Sherlock Holmes (2010) and Salidos de cuentas (2010), among others. In 2012, Downey reprized the role of Tony Stark in The Avengers.

The actor has sung on the soundtracks of some of his films, including: Chaplin, Too Much Sun, Two Girls and a Guy, Friends and Lovers, The Singing Detective, and Kiss Kiss Bang Bang. During 2001, he appeared in the music video for the Elton John song I Want Love. In 2004 he released a CD, The Futurist.
